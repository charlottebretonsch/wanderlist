Why Wanderlist?

Have you ever found yourself flying to another country to realise once you’re there that you’ve forgotten your toothbrush, phone charger or camera? I have. Many, many, many times.

Save money

You won’t have to spend money buying things you could have brought with you for free.

Save time

You won’t have to find a shop last minute, get there and look for a product in a unfamiliar setting.

Relax

You’ll feel confident that you’ll be prepared for your trip. It’s one less thing to think about, what a relief!
